Our Story

Founded in 2015, FreshSqueeze began with a simple mission: to provide the freshest, most nutritious juices made from locally-sourced organic fruits and vegetables.

Our cold-press process ensures maximum nutrient retention, giving you all the vitamins, minerals, and enzymes your body needs to thrive.

We believe in sustainability, health, and most importantly - great taste! That's why we never add preservatives, artificial flavors, or refined sugars to our products.
